CoolKids DayCare Centre

Is a centre that provides supervision, education and care for young children during daytime, particulary so that their parents can hold jobs.

Information

Vision

Coolkids DayCare aims high provides a safe and secure environment for young children. The centre enables them to develop mentally, physically, spiritually, and grow at their own pace.

Mission

Coolkid DayCare is striving provide an inclusive child care program to expose them to a committed diverse learning community. To prepare young children for future academic success. To help boost young children's confidence, by ensuring they are well guided.
